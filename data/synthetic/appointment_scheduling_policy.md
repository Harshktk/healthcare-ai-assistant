# Appointment Scheduling Policy

**Document type:** Operational policy
**Audience:** Patients and front-desk staff
**Last updated:** 2026-01-15
**Disclaimer:** This is a synthetic policy document created for an AI assistant
prototype. It does not represent any real healthcare provider.

## 1. Booking channels

Patients may schedule, reschedule, or cancel an appointment through any of the
following channels:

- The patient portal at `portal.example-clinic.com`
- The front-desk phone line, available Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM
- A walk-in request at the front desk
- A telehealth booking link sent by a clinician after a follow-up is recommended

Online booking is available for general medicine, paediatrics, dermatology, and
nutrition. Cardiology, neurology, and oncology appointments require front-desk
confirmation due to specialist availability.

## 2. New patient appointments

New patients must complete the registration form and upload a photo ID before
their first visit. New patient slots are 45 minutes long and are typically
available within 7 business days for general medicine and within 14 business
days for specialist consultations.

## 3. Follow-up appointments

Follow-up appointments are 20 minutes long. Patients are expected to book a
follow-up within the timeframe recommended by their clinician. If a follow-up
is delayed by more than 30 days from the recommended date, the visit will be
rebooked as a new consultation at the corresponding rate.

## 4. Cancellations and rescheduling

Cancellations and reschedules made at least 24 hours before the appointment
incur no fee. Cancellations within 24 hours, or no-shows, are charged a flat
fee of fifty rupees as a late-cancellation charge. The fee is waived for
documented medical emergencies.

## 5. Cardiology appointment availability

Cardiology consultations are available Monday, Wednesday, and Friday between
9:00 AM and 1:00 PM. Same-day cardiology appointments are not offered; the
earliest available cardiology slot is generally three to five business days
out. Emergency cardiac symptoms should be directed to the emergency department,
not to scheduled appointments.

## 6. Paediatric appointment availability

Paediatric appointments are available every weekday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Sick-child same-day slots are reserved each morning and released at 8:00 AM on
a first-come, first-served basis through the patient portal.
